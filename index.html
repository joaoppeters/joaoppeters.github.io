I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at EESC-USP and a Graduate Research and Teaching Assistant at the LACSEP Laboratory. My work focuses on the steady-state and transient analysis of electrical power system operations. My research interests include: power system stability and control; nonlinear systems analysis and control; power system planning and operation.
